# Resources

- [MoH Archive](https://www.moharchive.com){: target="_blank"}

    Place where you will find legacy websites dedicated to MoH as well as other materials we keep for history preservation purposes.
    
    The archives include:
 
      - xNULL forum
      - AAAA forum and mod database
      - The Mod Theater forum
      - .MAP website with mapping tutorials 

      and more!

- [WeBeHostiN](https://webehostin.com/games.php){: target="_blank"}

    If you want to host your own MoH:AA/SH/BT server without a hassle, you may want to consider using
    game hosting services provided by one of our community members - **own3mall**.

- [MoH Central](https://moh-central.net/){: target="_blank"}

    A community modding forum, previously also known as MRU (Mods-R-Us).

- [OpenMoHAA](https://www.openmohaa.org/){: target="_blank"}

    An open-source game engine compatible with Medal of Honor: Allied Assault and its expansions (Spearhead and Breakthrough).

- [MoH DB](https://www.moh-db.com/){: target="_blank"}

    Medal of Honor mods database. Think ModDB dedicated to Medal of Honor games.  

- [MoH League Discord](https://discord.gg/0oS4aqwv2H9KUJU6){: target="_blank"}

    Competitive MoH scene with regular tournaments, ladders and active clans.

- [Volute](https://volute.io/){: target="_blank"}

    Anti-cheat for MoH:AA used in tournaments.

- [HzM Modding](https://www.hazardmodding.com/){: target="_blank"}

    A modding group famous for its MoH Co-op mod and modding other Quake3-based
    games.

- [333networks](https://333networks.com/){: target="_blank"}

    A modding group famous for providing masterserver services compatible with GameSpy.

- [r/MOHAA](https://www.reddit.com/r/MOHAA/){: target="_blank"}

    Reddit channel for MoH enthusiasts.
