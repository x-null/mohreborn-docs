    Join our community!

    The MoH Reborn Project aims to revitalize the Medal of Honor classic games. Its corner stone is an unofficial MoH Reborn 1.12 patch that is developed for over 10 years!

    If you are interested in MoH modding, you play the game or host a server and want to keep in touch with like-minded people, then join our community Discord server!
